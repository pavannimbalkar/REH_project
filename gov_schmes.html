Government Schemes

Available Schemes

  • PM Kisan Yojana

    • Documents Required: Aadhaar Card, Bank Passbook, Land Documents.

    Processing Time: 15-30 days.

    How to Apply: Visit pmkisan.gov.in.

  • Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

    • Documents Required: Aadhaar Card, Ration Card.

    Processing Time: 7-14 days.

    How to Apply: Visit nrega.nic.in.
